NSW non-government school locations (2017)
This dataset contains comprehensive information for all non-government schools in NSW.

Data notes:

Data items include school locations, latitude and longitude coordinates, school type, affiliation, contact details and more.

The NSW non-government schools dataset is based on the current census of schools undertaken on the first Friday of August.

Data Source:

Statistics Unit. Centre for Education Statistics and Evaluation.
